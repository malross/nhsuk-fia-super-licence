To qualify for an FIA Super Licence, you must have accumulated at least 40 Super Licence points in the past three years of competition.

You must also have competed in at least 80% of races in two separate championship seasons.

How to earn points

Points can be earned in the following ways:

  • By finishing an eligible championship in a position that qualifies for Super Licence points
  • By driving in Formula 1 free practice sessions
  • By completing a full season without receiving any penalty points in a championship that uses the FIA penalty point system
