To qualify for an FIA Super Licence, you must have accumulated at least 40 Super Licence points in the past three years of competition.

How to earn points

Points can be earned in the following ways:

  • By completing at least 80% of races in an eligible championship
  • By driving in Formula 1 free practice sessions
  • By completing a full season without receiving any penalty points in a championship that uses the FIA penalty point system. Two points are awarded for each year that includes a penalty-free championship.
